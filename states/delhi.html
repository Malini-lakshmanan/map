Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant city known for its historical significance and modern infrastructure.

The city houses iconic landmarks such as India Gate, Qutub Minar, and the Red Fort.

Delhi is famous for its street food, including chaat, parathas, and kebabs.

It is a major educational hub with prestigious institutions like IIT Delhi and Delhi University.

Delhi also serves as the political center of India, hosting Parliament, the Supreme Court, and embassies.
