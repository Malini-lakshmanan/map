Karnataka

Karnataka is home to Bangalore, the IT capital of India, and a major technology hub.

The state has a rich history with ancient monuments like Hampi, Badami, and Mysore Palace.

It is famous for its diverse landscapes, from the Western Ghats to beautiful beaches in Mangalore and Gokarna.

Karnataka is a major producer of coffee and silk, contributing significantly to India's economy.

The state is also known for its traditional Carnatic music and famous temples like Udupi Krishna Temple.
